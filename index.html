Click the map or use your browser location to analyse rainfall at that point. The estimate follows the patch visible in recent radar frames and assumes near-term movement stays roughly steady.

About

This app does not forecast or model weather. It takes the (excellent) rainfall radar from met.ie and attempts to predict where the rain patches will be in the future, and whether they will pass over a certain point. For real weather modelling and prediction please support the work of Met Éireann and visit their site.
